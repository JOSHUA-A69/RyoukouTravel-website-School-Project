About Us


Ryoukou Travel is a website dedicated to showcasing the hidden gems and underrated tourist spots in Bohol, Philippines. Through curated guides and insightful articles, Ryoukou Travel aims to provide users with unique and off-the-beaten-path experiences, allowing them to explore Bohol beyond the typical tourist attractions. Whether it's secluded beaches, picturesque countryside landscapes, or charming local communities, Ryoukou Travel invites users to discover the lesser-known treasures of Bohol and immerse themselves in its rich culture and natural beauty.
